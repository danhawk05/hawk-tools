The workflow
Each tool handles one stage of turning rankings into traffic: find the queries worth chasing, diagnose the pages letting you down, fix the snippets losing clicks, and reinforce with the internal links you already have.
01 · FIND

Striking Distance

Surfaces the queries ranking just off the top three — positions 4–12 with real impressions — where a small push has the most click upside. Ranks every opportunity by estimated clicks gained, with brand terms stripped.

needs: GSC Queries export
Open tool → 02 · DIAGNOSE

Intent Mismatch Auditor

Finds pages that rank well but don't satisfy the visitor — strong position, collapsed click-through, and (with GA4) fast bounces. You're winning the ranking but losing the reader. Tells you which pages to rewrite to intent.

needs: GSC Pages export · GA4 optional
Open tool → 03 · FIX

Title Tag Doctor

Finds queries where you rank well but lose the click, then diagnoses why — weak title, SERP-feature crowding, or a snippet that doesn't stand out — and prescribes the fix. Prioritised by clicks lost each month.

needs: GSC Queries export
Open tool → 04 · REINFORCE

Internal Link Reallocator

Pairs your strong pages with striking-distance targets on the same topic, so you push struggling pages up using authority you already have. Surfaces the internal links worth adding, ranked by donor strength.

needs: GSC Pages export

Getting your data

  1. In Google Search Console, open Performance → Search results, set your date range, and click Export → Excel.
  2. The export has separate Queries and Pages tabs — each tool reads whichever it needs.
  3. For the Intent Mismatch Auditor's engagement view, also export Pages and screens from GA4 (Reports → Engagement).
  4. Drop the file into the tool. It's parsed in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere.
